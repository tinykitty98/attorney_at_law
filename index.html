Meet Your Advocate

“I’m not a physician, but clients have thanked me for saving their lives.”

The most satisfying moments of James’ career were when clients expressed that level of gratitude for his legal work. He spent many nights and weekends working on petitions to release non-violent prisoners from life sentences. His efforts resulted in dozens of men and women getting a second chance at life.

James brings that same zeal to custody, criminal, divorce, and personal injury cases. He has won at the Ohio Supreme Court, the 6th Circuit Court of Appeals, and the First District Court of Appeals. You’ll find his name on multi-million-dollar verdicts for personal injury cases. He has obtained not guilty verdicts in state, federal, and military courts. His courtroom litigation work has forced millions in settlements prior to trial, including a recent six-figure civil rights settlement against the Cincinnati Police.

James is especially protective of clients in criminal and custody matters. Because of his length of service and success in the courtroom, he is able to charge lower rates than many in the community while maintaining a reasonable caseload so each client receives the attention they need and deserve.
